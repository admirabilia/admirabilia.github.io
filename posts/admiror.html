Three years ago, I realized that a lot of images that I like online end up being forgotten, buried under the novelty of more recent likes. Thats when I decided to start printing a collection of my favorite image of the month. At the end of the month, I go to the list of likes and select nine images. I did it for three or four months then I stopped, but I started again last month. I still plan to print a copy to hang in my room but also publish an online version. The name of the posts containing these collections will have a YYYYMM naming convention.The first online collection is 202603
